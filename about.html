> Hi there, I'm Vivian

I am a junior Front-end developed based in Tampa, Florida. I create simple yet visually appealing and user-friendly digital experiences.

Growing up, I was always immersed in some type of art, whether I was drawing, painting, playing musical instruments, or photography. My interest in digital technology also grew, manifesting in activities like playing design games, making aesthetic school presentations, and editing photos and videos.

Previously, I worked as a behavioral therapist for children with ASD. Transitioning from this position to a developer has been a journey of growth and transformation for me. In that role, I honed skills in communication, problem-solving, and empathy while assisting individuals with diverse needs and backgrounds. Every day presented unique challenges that required adaptability and resourcefulness. Upon devling into the world of technology, I recognized the parallels between my experiences in behavioral therapy and the principles of coding. Both fields require meticulous attention to detail, the ability to deconstruct complex tasks, and a strong commitment to continous learning and improvement. Drawing from my background in behavioral therapy, I embraced coding with a similar mindset, viewing challenges as opportunities for personal and professional development. Through self-directed learning, online courses, and hands-on projects, I established a strong foundation in programming languages, web development frameworks, and software methodologies.

How did I get into software development?

My diverse experiences have taught me the profound significance of personal interactions and experiences. I've chosen to pursue a this career path where I can merge my passions and background in psychology in a new purposeful way. With a focus on creating thoughtful and intuitive experiences, I aim to empower users, foster connections, and enhance the way they interact with technology, ultimately enriching their daily lives.
